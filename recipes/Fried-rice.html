Fried Rice

Description

Fried Rice is a colorful and delicious dish made with cooked rice, vegetables, proteins, and seasoned to perfection. It's a versatile and popular meal in many cultures.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked rice
  • 1/2 cup diced carrots
  • 1/2 cup green peas
  • 1/2 cup diced chicken or liver
  • Onion, oil, and seasoning

Steps

  1. Stir-fry onions, vegetables, and protein in oil.
  2. Add rice and mix thoroughly.
  3. Add seasoning and fry for a few more minutes.
  4. Serve hot and enjoy.
